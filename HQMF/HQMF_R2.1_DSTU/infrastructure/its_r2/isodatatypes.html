 1Notice to Balloters

Any comments provided may receive future consideration.

 2ISO 21090 Healthcare Datatypes

The ISO-Harmonized data types are effectively the XML ITS Data Types R2. They will replace the XML ITS Data Types R1. The format differences between the specification provided here and conventional HL7 specifications arise because of the need to align with ISO style requirements.

The ISO-Harmonized data types document is a shared document between HL7 and ISO. The final format of this document is as a full ISO specification (it is identified as ISO/FDIS 21090:2009(E)). This document is the result of a jointly-balloted document by HL7, ISO, and CEN in a ballot that opened on Mar 29, 2008 and closed on Aug 29, 2008 (5 months, per ISO and CEN rules). Ballot Disposition was conducted at both HL7 and ISO meetings through 2008 and 2009.

The linked ISO-Harmonized Data Types, Release 1 document is a pdf: ISO Datatypes Standard.pdf (for HL7 Normative Ballot) (Hint: right-click and select 'Save Target As' to download, or 'Open in New Window'.)

Because the material would become an ISO standard, the ITS Work Group petitioned the Technical Steering Committee to move the document from DSTU to full Normative ballot and that petition was granted.

Please note that the reconciliation spreadsheet from the September 2009 ballot has been posted on the Ballot Desktop.
